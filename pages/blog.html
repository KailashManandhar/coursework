Information Technology

In today’s world technology has been a very important part of human life, technology plays very important role in our daily life and business in both positive and negative way.

E-commerce technology is all about using the internet to buy and sell products. It includes all types of products. This process includes websites, platforms, and mobile apps. This technology has brought significant growth in business and people who buy and sell things online. It is really easy and convenient for many people who do not like to go stores. Altogether, technology helps business work faster, operate more freely, and reach customers worldwide.

Good Things about Technology in E-Commerce

With the help of technology customers from different places can easily shop whenever they want through smartphone, laptops, without visiting the physical store which helps to save time and people can easily shop even with their busy schedule. This prove to be great benefit to busy people and anyone who lives in distant places.

E commerce business deals with hundred of products all over the world. people are able to sell their products to different cities ,countries starting with the local buyers to large e commerce industry. with the help of technology businesses have grown quickly all over the world.

Running an online store can be cheaper than opening a physical shop as it can save your rent, utilities, and labor cost. Shopping on online store also help customer save their travel expenses to visit stores.

Technology enables product reviews, chatbots, and helps businesses understand customer preferences and market demand.

Dark Sides of Technology in E-Commerce

Shopping online may require personal and financial details which may sometimes lead to cybercrimes like hacking or loss of money. Technical issues like system breakdowns or website crashes can interrupt services. Customers may face product dissatisfaction since they cannot try products before purchasing.

E commerce businesses mainly rely on the internet, digital systems. Any technical failure can lead to downfall of the business, As the technology is growing in such a vast way that AI and self service technologies can cause job displacement of humans which may lead to unemployment.

Access use of technology has also increased more number of crimes like fake websites, fake products, scams which leads to loose trust of customers in trusting and buying products on online platforms.

How Technology Boosts Sales

  1. Technology enables businesses to market goods online via websites and mobile applications.
  2. Digital marketing attracts more customers through social media and online ads, increasing sales.
  3. Customers can buy goods quickly and easily through online payment systems.
  4. Technology helps businesses understand customer needs using data, information, and feedback.
  5. Smart systems and automation save time and enhance sales.

Sustainable Tips and Tricks

Firstly we must adopt the concept of reduce, reuse, and recycle. Adopting this concept means reducing harm from the environment and creating a better future .Instead of buying products made from different harmful plastics, chemicals etc ,we can try buying products made from recycled materials. Participating in different environmental campaigns , green programs and planting trees which helps improving air quality, reduces heat. Sustainable living is all about simple living, mindful choices. Your small action can make big differences.

Interview: Sarah Green, Community Environmental Advocate

Q: What is it that made you attracted towards a better future of the environment?

A: During my trip to Kathmandu, I saw how plastic waste and pollution affected the city and rivers. I got sick because the air was so polluted. Seeing this serious problem, my inner mind decided to contribute to this problem and save our environment for a better future.

Q: How would you encourage people to convert their day-to-day life and environment into a healthy lifestyle?

A: Organize awareness programs in schools and colleges, environmental campaigns, social media awareness, and promote sustainable products like reusable bags and bamboo items instead of plastic.

Q: Communities and their contribution to sustainability?

A: Communities hold important power in promoting sustainability. Collaboration accelerates work. Programs like cleaning, tree-planting, and campaigns spread positive impact faster.
